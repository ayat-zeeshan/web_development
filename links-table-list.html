The four seasons in order are spring, summer, autumn (or fall), and winter. This annual cycle is driven by the Earth's tilt relative to the sun, with each season lasting approximately three months based on astronomical or meteorological definitions.

Serial Season name Duration
1. Spring March – May (Northern Hemisphere)
2. Summer June – August (Northern Hemisphere)
3. Autumn September – November (Northern Hemisphere)
4. Winter December – February (Northern Hemisphere)
