Who are we?

We are the 'Blue Tiger Titans,' one of the four benchball teams from Personality Pathway 1. We focus on improving and giving our 100% in every game. Our strengths include: Orgnisation, Teamwork, Tactics and Resilience. Every lesson is taken as a chance to develop different skills like attack and defense.

In addition, we ensure that everyone has a role and is participating. This helps the team build a bond and void creating an atmosphere of neglection. It is vital to make sure that nobody feels left out.Furthermore, Blue Tiger Titans prefer to keep a good relationship with other teams to prevent any fights and instead, set an example of good sportsmanship.
