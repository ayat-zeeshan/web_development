Welcome to Upton Court Grammar School

The school values its tradition of excellence and honours all of those who have, through their academic credentials and personal commitment, become outstanding members of the community and role models for the next generation. At the forefront of education for more than a century, Upton Court Grammar School successfully blends tradition with a forward-thinking, internationally-minded educational philosophy that truly meets the needs of young people venturing into adulthood in an increasingly complex world. If you have a question, please contact your child’s tutor or Head of Year. You can email office@uptoncourtgrammar.org.uk and indicate who you would like your email forwarded to. For example: ‘For the attention of the Head of Year 7’. All other enquiries should be sent to our school receptionist by emailing office@uptoncourtgrammar.org.uk or calling 01753522892.
